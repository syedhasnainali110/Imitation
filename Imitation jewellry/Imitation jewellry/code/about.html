About

Where History Meets Craftsmanship

At Syed Jewellery, we specialize in creating unique, handcrafted rings from genuine coins, preserving the intricate details and the story behind each one. Our mission is to celebrate history and provide our customers with meaningful, high-quality jewellery that stands the test of time. Whether you’re a collector, a history enthusiast, or someone who values unique accessories, our coin rings are designed to tell a story as unique as the person wearing them.In addition to our exceptional coin rings, we also offer a curated selection of watches, expertly crafted for style and durability. Our range of jewellery repairs, including ring and bracelet restoration, silver and gold soldering, and custom modifications, ensures your treasured pieces are maintained with the utmost care.With a commitment to craftsmanship, customer satisfaction, and preserving the legacy of every coin, we are proud to share our work with customers around the world. Thank you for choosing Empire Jewellery – where history meets elegance and tradition blends with modern style.
